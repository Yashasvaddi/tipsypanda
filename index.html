Our Story

A journey of flavor, passion, and culinary excellence

Tipsy Panda was established in 2017 in Virar (Northern Mumbai Suburb) and since then has made a name in the local food and beverage scene. Known for its varied offerings of Indo-Chinese inspired dishes, Tipsy Panda is a go-to name in almost all households when it comes to taste, hygiene, consistency, customer service and affordability.

Tipsy Panda now has 3 successfully running restaurants in Vasai(West), Vasai(East) and Virar(West). The brand Tipsy Panda is ready to expand its wings with the launch of the cloud kitchen franchise model "Tipsy Panda Express".
